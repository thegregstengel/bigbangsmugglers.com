A reference index of everything you will encounter in the galaxy.

## Ships

Ships are divided into Federation and Pirate lines, six roles, five tiers each.

### Federation

**Trading** — high cargo, light weapons
- Nebula Merchant (Tier 1)
- CoreStar Freighter (Tier 2)
- TriStar Hauler (Tier 3)
- Vanguard Trader (Tier 4)
- Federation Galleon (Tier 5)

**War** — heavy weapons, low cargo
- Patrol Corvette (Tier 1)
- Strike Frigate (Tier 2)
- Battle Cruiser (Tier 3)
- Enforcer Dreadnought (Tier 4)
- Federation Battleship (Tier 5)

**Balanced** — moderate everything
- Frontier Scout (Tier 1)
- Pathfinder (Tier 2)
- Vanguard Ranger (Tier 3)
- Horizon Cruiser (Tier 4)
- Federation Odyssey (Tier 5)

### Pirate

**Smuggling** — high cargo, best escape rating
- Shadow Runner (Tier 1)
- Black Market Hauler (Tier 2)
- Plunder Barge (Tier 3)
- Marauder's Fortune (Tier 4)
- Pirate Leviathan (Tier 5)

**Raider** — heavy fighters and torpedoes, low cargo
- Cutthroat (Tier 1)
- Reaver (Tier 2)
- Warlord (Tier 3)
- Blood Talon (Tier 4)
- Pirate Juggernaut (Tier 5)

**Corsair** — balanced with highest escape rating
- Rogue's Gambit (Tier 1)
- Freebooter (Tier 2)
- Blackheart (Tier 3)
- Crimson Eclipse (Tier 4)
- Pirate Dominion (Tier 5)

## Starports

Every starport has six places inside. Names differ by faction, services are identical.

### Federation Starport Places
- Federation Reserve Bank
- TriStar Shipyard and Maintenance Bay
- Nebula Ship Sales and Acquisitions
- Federation Contract Office
- Vanguard Colonial Recruitment
- CoreStar Tactical Supply

### Pirate Haven Places
- The Vault
- Blackheart Shipbreakers
- Prize Vessels and Salvage
- The Bounty Board
- Crimson Crew Recruitment
- Black Market Supply

## Ports

Standard ports appear throughout the galaxy for trading and resupply. Port type reflects the local economy and faction alignment.

- **Depot** — fuel and basic supply stop
- **Agricultural Station** — organics-focused trading post
- **Tech Hub** — equipment and technology exchange
- **Mining Station** — raw resource outpost
- **Research Complex** — science and exploration support
- **Pirate Base** — pirate-aligned supply and trade
- **Black Market** — off-the-books exchange in pirate territory
- **Trading Post** — player-built port on an owned planet

---

## Planets

Planets appear in sectors throughout the galaxy and can be claimed and developed.

- **Habitable World** — temperate, capable of supporting large populations
- **Resource Planet** — mineral-rich, high production potential
- **Gas Giant** — massive, inhospitable, and visually striking
- **Barren World** — sparse and unforgiving, but claimable

---

## Landmarks

Landmarks are fixed points of interest with no functional gameplay role. They mark the galaxy and give it character.

- **Black Hole** — extreme gravitational anomaly
- **Ancient Ruins** — remnants of something that came before
- **Navigation Beacon** — automated waypoint marker
- **Space Anomaly** — unexplained spatial phenomenon

---

## Stations

Stations are structures serving specific functions in a sector.

- **Defense Station** — armed platform for territorial control
- **Repair Station** — field maintenance for passing ships
- **Research Station** — scientific outpost in deep space

---

## Hazards

Hazards are environmental sector features. They do not interact directly with gameplay but shape the character of the space around them.

- **Nebula** — dense gas cloud reducing visibility
- **Asteroid Field** — rocky debris field
- **Radiation Zone** — high-energy emission area
