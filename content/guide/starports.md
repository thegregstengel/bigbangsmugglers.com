# Starports

Starports are advanced facilities offering services beyond standard ports. They're your hub for upgrades, banking, ship purchases, missions, and recruitment.

## Finding Starports

### Federation Starports
- **Location:** Federation space (first 5% of sectors)
- **Names:** Federation Starbase Alpha, Beta, etc.
- **Theme:** Corporate, clean, organized

### Pirate Havens
- **Location:** Pirate territories (~5% of sectors, randomly clustered)
- **Names:** Pirate Stronghold Shadow, Viper, etc.
- **Theme:** Criminal, rough, underground

### Faction Parity
Both factions offer **identical services** with different branding:

| Federation | Pirate Haven | Service |
|-----------|--------------|---------|
| Federation Reserve Bank | The Vault | Banking |
| TriStar Shipyard | Blackheart Shipbreakers | Ship services |
| Nebula Ship Sales | Prize Vessels | Ship purchases |
| Contract Office | Bounty Board | Missions |
| Vanguard Recruitment | Crimson Crew | Personnel |
| CoreStar Supply | Black Market | Equipment |

## The Six Places

Each starport has 6 "places" - specialized facilities for different services.

### üè¶ Banking
**Federation Reserve Bank / The Vault**

Store credits safely to protect against loss (future combat).

**Services:**
- **Deposit** - Store credits in your account
- **Withdraw** - Take credits back to ship
- **Balance** - View banked credits

**Why use it:**
- Safe storage (can't be stolen)
- Passive interest (future)
- Required for large purchases

---

### üîß Shipyard
**TriStar Shipyard / Blackheart Shipbreakers**

Ship maintenance and upgrade hub.

**Services:**

1. **Rename** (1,000 credits)
   - Change your ship's name
   - Personal touch

2. **Repair** (variable cost)
   - Full restoration: hull, shields, torpedoes to 100%
   - Cheaper than field repairs
   - One-click full service

3. **Warp Drive**
   - **Install:** 50,000 credits (Level 1)
   - **Upgrade:** 15,000 per level (up to Level 5)
   - Faster warps, better efficiency

4. **Weapons Systems** (Exponential cost: base √ó 1.4^level)
   - **Fighters:** Increase fighter capacity
   - **Torpedoes:** Increase torpedo capacity
   - Each upgrade adds +10 to base capacity

5. **Cargo Holds** (Exponential cost: base √ó 1.4^level)
   - Increase cargo capacity
   - More profit per trading run
   - Essential for traders

### üöÄ Ship Sales
**Nebula Ship Sales / Prize Vessels**

Buy new ships. Trade-in your current ship toward the purchase.

**Ship Catalog:**
- **15 Federation ships** - Trading, War, Balanced classes
- **15 Pirate ships** - Smuggling, Raider, Corsair classes
- **5 tiers per class** - Level 2 through Level 6 ships

**Ship Roles:**

| Federation | Pirate | Focus |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Trading | Smuggling | High cargo, light weapons |
| War | Raider | Heavy weapons, moderate cargo |
| Balanced | Corsair | Moderate everything |

**Pricing:**
- Tier 1 (Level 2): 5,000 credits
- Tier 2 (Level 3): 15,000 credits
- Tier 3 (Level 4): 40,000 credits
- Tier 4 (Level 5): 100,000 credits
- Tier 5 (Level 6): 250,000 credits

**Purchase Process:**
1. Browse ships by role (grouped display)
2. View stats (shields, cargo, fighters, torpedoes)
3. See stock availability
4. Confirm purchase (trade-in old ship)

### üìã Missions
**Contract Office / The Bounty Board**

Accept and manage missions.

**Features:**
- View **Available Missions** - All offered missions
- View **Active Mission** - Your current mission (1 max)
- **Accept** - Take on a mission
- **Abandon** - Drop your active mission (no penalty)

**Mission Types:**
- Trading - Deliver cargo
- Combat - Destroy enemies
- Exploration - Scout sectors
- Diplomacy - Deliver messages

See the [Missions Guide](../missions) for full details.

### üë• Recruitment
**Vanguard Colonial Recruitment / Crimson Crew Recruitment**

Hire personnel for your ship.

**Personnel Types:**

1. **Fighters** - 500 credits each
   - Combat craft for defense and offense
   - Used in combat encounters
   - Restocked after battles

2. **Workers** - 300 credits each
   - Crew for ship operations (future)
   - Mining, salvage, production
   - Colony support

**Purchase:**
- Select quantity
- Confirm purchase
- Personnel added to ship immediately

---

### üì¶ Tactical Supply
**CoreStar Tactical Supply / Black Market Supply**

Purchase deployable equipment.

**Equipment Types:**

1. **Navigation Beacons** - 2,500 credits each
   - Mark sectors for navigation
   - Share locations with allies (future)
   - Territory control markers

2. **Proximity Mines** - 5,000 credits each
   - Defensive weapons
   - Deploy in sectors to damage enemies (future)

**Purchase:**
- Select item and quantity
- Confirm purchase

- Deploy from Nav or Combat screen (future)

## Using Starports

### First Visit
1. **Nav screen** - Tap sector with starport
2. **Warp** to that sector
3. **Starport icon** appears in sector view
4. **Tap Starport** to open directory

### Directory Screen
- Lists all 6 "places"
- Tap any place to open its modal
- Each place shows icon, name, description
- Modal opens full-screen for that service

### Service Flow
1. Open place modal
2. Use service (deposit, upgrade, purchase, etc.)
3. Confirm action
4. See result and updated stats
5. Close modal to return to directory

## Starport Strategy

### Banking
- Deposit excess credits for safety
- Keep working capital on ship for trades
- Withdraw for large purchases

### Upgrades
- **Early game:** Cargo holds (trading profit)
- **Mid game:** Warp drive (efficiency)
- **Late game:** Weapons (combat capability)

### Ship Purchases
- Upgrade every 2-3 tiers (don't overextend)
- Match ship to playstyle (trading vs combat)
- Save credits before upgrading

### Missions
- Accept missions aligned with your activities
- Trading missions fit trading routes
- Combat missions require capable ship

### Recruitment & Supply
- Keep fighters stocked for combat
- Workers useful for future features
- Beacons for marking territory
- Mines for defensive control

## Tips

- Visit starports regularly for maintenance
- Repair cheaper here than at standard ports
- Banking protects against future piracy/loss
- Ship upgrades compound over time
- Faction doesn't matter - services are identical
- Plan upgrades around your playstyle
- Don't drain all credits - keep reserves
