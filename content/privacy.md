Protovision Games ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play Big Bang Smugglers (the "Game").

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Display name** — the name shown to other players in the game
- **Email address** — used for account authentication and recovery

### Game Data
As you play, we collect and store:
- Game progress, including your ship, credits, sector location, and player statistics
- In-game actions such as trades, combat, missions, and galaxy activity
- Galaxy membership and corporation (corp) affiliations

### Technical Data
We automatically collect:
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Crash reports and error logs (used to identify and fix bugs)
- General usage data (session length, feature usage)

We do **not** collect your real name, phone number, payment information, or precise location.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Operate and maintain the Game and its servers
- Authenticate your account and keep it secure
- Save and sync your game progress across sessions
- Display your display name to other players in shared galaxy environments
- Identify and fix crashes and bugs
- Improve game features and balance based on usage patterns
- Communicate important game updates or account information (via email, if necessary)

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## Shared and Multiplayer Features

Big Bang Smugglers is a multiplayer game. By playing, you understand that:
- Your **display name** and **in-game statistics** (XP, rank, ship class, corporation) are visible to other players in your galaxy
- In-game actions such as combat, trading, and galaxy events may appear in the in-game feed visible to other players

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Game:

### Google Firebase
We use Firebase (provided by Google) for authentication, database storage, server functions, and crash reporting. Firebase may collect technical and usage data in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

- Firebase Authentication
- Cloud Firestore (game data storage)
- Cloud Functions (server-side game logic)
- Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting)
- Firebase Analytics (usage analytics)

## Data Retention

We retain your account and game data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal information and game data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

To request account deletion, contact us at the email address below.

## Children's Privacy

Big Bang Smugglers is intended for players **13 years of age and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
