## How We Prioritize

Our development priorities are driven by:

1. **Core Gameplay**: Features that make the game fun and engaging
2. **Player Feedback**: What our alpha testers request most
3. **Technical Foundations**: Systems that enable future features
4. **Polish & UX**: Making existing features feel great
