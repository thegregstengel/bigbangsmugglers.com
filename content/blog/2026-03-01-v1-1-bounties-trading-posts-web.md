Version 1.1 is a big one. Three major systems landed alongside a new way to play.

## Bounty System

Players can now post bounties on other players. Head to any tavern, put up credits, and watch the galaxy's hunters take notice. Bounties scale with the reward and show up in the feed when they're collected. The system ties into combat -- taking down a wanted player pays out automatically.

## Player-Built Trading Posts

Own a planet? You can now construct a trading post on it. Trading posts let you sell commodities to other players at prices you influence, and you earn a cut of every transaction. Build it, stock it from your planet's storage, and upgrade it over time to increase capacity and revenue. Other players can rob your trading post too, so keep your garrison healthy.

## play.bigbangsmugglers.com

The game is now playable in a browser at [play.bigbangsmugglers.com](https://play.bigbangsmugglers.com). Same game, same account, same galaxy -- no install required. Invite codes are required for the web version during the current alpha period.

## Past Seasons

The Feed screen now has a Past Seasons tab. Completed galaxies are preserved with final standings so you can look back at how previous seasons played out.

## Bug Fixes and Polish

- Auto-repair now triggers correctly at turn reset boundaries
- Production ticks aligned to 4-hour UTC boundaries (matching turn resets)
- Planet production correctly resets the ready flag after collection
- NPC combat feed no longer leaks private battle results to other players
- Ship upgrade sync fixed -- holds, shields, fighters, and torps all update correctly after purchase
- Pirate ports no longer offer standard trading (blackmarket and tavern only)
- Turn reset display shows the correct countdown to the next 4-hour boundary
