v1.12.2 is a stability and accuracy pass across seasons, trading, combat, and exploration.

## Season Setup Accuracy

Season setup screens now display correct counts, combat stats, and Past Seasons data. Previously, certain values could drift out of sync — this release ensures the numbers you see match your actual season record.

## Trading and Pirate Combat Fixes

Edge cases in trading and pirate combat that could mis-handle cargo quantities or credit totals have been resolved. Transactions and combat loot now settle correctly in all scenarios.

## Trader Reputation and Upgrade Displays

Trader reputation and upgrade progress screens now reflect accurate pricing and requirement data. What you see in the UI matches your actual player data — no more mismatches between displayed costs and real values.

## Visited-Sector History

Your visited-sector history now includes subtle territory context, making it easier to recall where you have been and which faction controls the space around you.
