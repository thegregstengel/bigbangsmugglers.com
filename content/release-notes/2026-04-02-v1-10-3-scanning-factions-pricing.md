v1.10.3 brings faster scanning, faction-driven economics, and a round of scan reliability fixes.

## Instant Scan Sector Results

Sector scans now return results immediately with richer detail. The bridge flow is faster — no waiting, no extra taps. You scan, you see what's out there.

## Faction Reputation Pricing

Your faction standing now directly affects your wallet. Ports controlled by factions you're aligned with offer discounts on trades and purchases. Hostile factions mark prices up. NPC traders follow the same rules — reputation matters everywhere you spend credits.

## Deployable Scan Fixes

Deployable scans were deducting turn costs inconsistently and sometimes failing to display results. Both issues are fixed — turn costs are now deducted correctly and results show up reliably.

## Clearer Pricing Labels

Port purchase screens now show faction pricing effects explicitly. If a discount or markup is in play, you'll see it labeled before you commit to the transaction.

## Stealth Reconnaissance Removed

Stealth Reconnaissance has been temporarily removed from the Ship Bridge while the revised scan flow ships. It will return in a future update.

## Overall

v1.10.3 makes scanning faster, trading fairer, and pricing more transparent. Faction reputation now has real economic weight across the game.
