v1.17.1 is a small hotfix focused on toast notification dismissal. Toasts can now be cleared on demand instead of lingering on screen until they auto-expire.

## Swipe to Dismiss

Toast notifications can now be dismissed immediately with swipe gestures instead of forcing players to wait for them to expire.

Swipe in any direction to clear a toast the moment it appears, so notifications never block your view during gameplay.

## Tap to Dismiss

Tap-to-dismiss was also added for faster cleanup when a toast is in the way.

A single tap anywhere on the toast clears it instantly, giving players a second quick option alongside the swipe gesture.

## Frontend-Only Fix

This was a frontend-only fix with no backend or gameplay-state changes. Your account, ships, credits, and progress are completely untouched by this update.
