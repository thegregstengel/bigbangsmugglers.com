v1.5.4 is a quality-of-life update with two improvements.

## Max Buttons on Trading Screens

Buy and sell screens now have a Max button next to the quantity input. Tapping it fills the quantity with the most you can buy (limited by your credits and free cargo space) or the most you can sell (your full stack). You can still adjust the number before confirming. Max buttons are on the standard trading screen, the black market, and trading posts.

## Toast Notification Positioning

The notification toasts introduced in v1.5.2 were appearing too close to the status bar at the top of the screen. They now appear roughly 20% down from the top, keeping them clear of the system UI while staying easy to see.
