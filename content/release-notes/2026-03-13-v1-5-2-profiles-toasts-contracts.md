v1.5.2 is a quality-of-life update with three notable improvements.

## Player Profiles

Players now have public profile cards showing lifetime stats — kills, deaths, missions completed, trades, sectors explored, credits earned, and current streak. Tap a player name on the leaderboard to see their profile. Your own stats are visible in the Settings screen with additional detail only you can see.

## Themed Notifications

The game's system alerts have been replaced with a custom notification system that fits the space theme. Success messages appear in teal, errors in red, warnings in yellow, and event notifications in gold. Notifications stack at the top of the screen, animate in and out, and can be dismissed with a swipe. Confirmation dialogs are also styled to match the game rather than using the plain system modal.

## Ship Contracts Tab

The Contracts tab on the Ship screen now shows all four types of bounty activity alongside your missions: faction bounties you can collect, bounties posted on you (shown with a red WANTED label), and bounties you've posted on other players. Previously only missions were shown here.
