v1.3.2 fixes the battle recap screen not appearing after hitting Attack on an NPC encounter.

The recap was being dismissed before it could render. Now it shows immediately when you hit Attack, stays on screen until you close it, and then refreshes your ship and sector state.

Combined with the move speed improvements in v1.3.1, NPC combat is now fully instant end-to-end.
