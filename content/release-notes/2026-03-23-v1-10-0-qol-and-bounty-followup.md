v1.10.0 is a quality-of-life and progression update that builds on the recent corporation, bounty, and faction systems.

This release improves visibility, tracking, and pricing behavior across several major systems and closes out a few long-running follow-up items.

## Tappable Corp Cards on Leaderboards

Corporation cards on the leaderboard are now interactive, making it easier to inspect corporation information from the leaderboard flow instead of treating it as static display data.

## Player Platform Tracking and Version Gating

The game now tracks player platform details and supports stronger version-aware behavior.

This lays the groundwork for cleaner forced-update handling and better release management across different clients.

## Most Wanted & Top Bounty Hunters Leaderboard

Bounty-related progression now has better visibility through leaderboard support.

This helps turn the new bounty systems into something players can actually compete around instead of leaving them as hidden background mechanics.

## Reputation-Scaled Pricing

Faction alignment now affects pricing more broadly and more consistently.

Friendly faction relationships improve costs and hostile relationships make services and purchases more expensive, reinforcing the idea that reputation should have real economic impact.

## Overall

v1.10.0 strengthens several systems that were already moving in the right direction: corporations are easier to inspect, version/platform handling is more robust, bounty competition is more visible, and faction reputation now carries more meaningful economic consequences.
