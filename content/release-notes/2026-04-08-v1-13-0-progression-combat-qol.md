v1.13.0 is a quality-of-life release focused on progression clarity, cleaner combat feedback, and fewer frustrating edge cases in trading, movement, and corporation flows.

## Cargo Hold Milestones

Cargo hold upgrades now use a clearer five-tier milestone system.

This makes ship progression easier to understand at a glance and gives hold growth a more structured sense of advancement instead of feeling like a flat list of incremental purchases.

## Trading, Wallet, and Upgrade Accuracy

Several economy and upgrade edge cases were cleaned up in this release.

Purchases now validate against the right credits, upgrade requirement screens show your current values more accurately, and planet production messaging is clearer about where resources actually go.

## Combat and Movement Reliability

Combat and movement flows are more stable across several frustrating edge cases.

Pirate combat resolution is more reliable, ambush encounters do a better job surfacing combat recap information, and disabled ships can no longer warp away when they should be stranded.

## Reputation and Corporation Flow Improvements

Faction reputation is now easier to inspect during normal play.

Instead of living behind a separate tab, reputation details now appear in the Stats screen, and corporation screens now stay in the correct state after you create or join a corp.

## Tech Hub and Escape Pod Fixes

This release also includes cleanup to Tech Hub upgrade behavior and escape pod handling.

Those fixes reduce inconsistencies in late-session progression and recovery moments so ship systems behave more predictably when something goes wrong.
