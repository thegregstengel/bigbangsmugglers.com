v1.17.1 is a small hotfix focused on toast notification dismissal and polish.

## Swipe to Dismiss

Toast notifications can now be dismissed immediately with swipe gestures instead of forcing players to wait for them to expire. Drag a toast horizontally in either direction to clear it from the screen.

## Tap to Dismiss

A close button on every toast gives quick single-tap dismissal for faster cleanup when a notification is in the way.

## No Gameplay Changes

This was a frontend-only fix. No backend or gameplay-state changes were required.

Players should have a smoother moment-to-moment UX around sync and feedback toasts because notifications can now be cleared on demand instead of lingering on screen.
