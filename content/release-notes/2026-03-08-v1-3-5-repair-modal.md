v1.3.5 is a small polish release.

## Styled Repair Complete Screen

The plain gray system dialog that appeared after repairing your ship at a starport has been replaced with a proper in-game screen. It now shows exactly what was restored (fighters, shields, torpedoes) and what it cost, including bank fees if applicable.

## Feed Highlights Trimmed

"Your Recent Highlights" in the Galaxy Feed now shows one item instead of five. It was too noisy.
